ABOUT CONTACT LESSONS












Our Goal


Our goal is to give simple solutions to daily problems or situations that people do not usually learn in school. We provide these solutions through the form of videos, articles, and more. Some websites have distractions which prevent people from focusing on what they want to learn. Our website has lessons that entertains and educates the user.
We are from Girls Who Code.
Our two final weeks of our program required us to make a project that solves a real world solution. At first, we were just girls who had multiple ideas but couldn't choose one. Our instructor then called us Blank Canvas because we had nothing in mind. Finally after many discussions, we concluded to make a website that teaches people things that they wouldn't learn in school. That Website became known as Home 101.
